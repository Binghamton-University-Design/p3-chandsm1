Highlight: Mallory Braun

These mini sketckbooks are completely handmade with reused materials, making them each utterly unique and also sustainable.

Braun creates these handsewn booklets using leftover junk mail envelopes, wrapping paper, shopping bag tissue paper, threads, ribbons, and more. Her collaging technique puts leftover papers to great use, while giving someone a one of a kind little gem.
